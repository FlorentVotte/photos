Day 04

Lost in Arashiyama

We woke up before dawn to catch the first light hitting the bamboo grove. The towering stalks swayed gently in the wind, creating a sound unlike anything else—a soft, wooden clattering that felt almost musical. As the sun rose, piercing through the dense green canopy, the entire grove lit up in an ethereal glow.

Later, we wandered along the Katsura River, watching boats drift lazily downstream. It was a moment of perfect stillness in a trip filled with movement.
