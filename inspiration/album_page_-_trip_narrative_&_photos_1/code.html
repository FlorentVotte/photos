Kyoto in Autumn

October 2023 • Japan

keyboard_arrow_down
format_quote

The air was crisp as we stepped off the train. Kyoto greeted us not with the bustle of Tokyo, but with a quiet, ancient dignity.

We spent days wandering through narrow alleys where the scent of incense lingered in the air. This collection captures the fleeting moments of gold and crimson that define the season, a visual meditation on the passage of time in the old capital.

Golden Reflections

Kinkaku-ji, the Golden Pavilion, shimmering in the afternoon sun.

Beyond the city limits, the mountains called. We took a train to Arashiyama, where the bamboo groves filter the sunlight into a green haze. It felt like stepping into another world, one governed by silence and the rustling of leaves.
